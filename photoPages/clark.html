James William "Buck" Clark

b. 1862 ?Feliciana, Graves Co KY d. 1940 near Bowie, TX. Principal genetic ancestor, maternal great grandfather. Southern thoroughbred descended from British gentry on both father and mother (THOMAS, his strongly dominant bloodline) sides. Direct paternal descendant of colonial Virginia planter Captain Christopher Clark (son of Micajah And Sallie Ann MOORMAN CLARK, b. ca 1681 VA) and Penelope ?BOLLING.

Samuel Clemens aka "Mark Twain"

Maternal ?double Cousin on Clark and Moorman lines. Paternal 2nd great grandmother was Agnes, daughter of Captain Christopher Clark and Penelope ?Bolling. ?Most intelligent American of 19th century and author of one of greatest American novels, "Huckleberry Finn".

Pat Boone

Daniel Boone
